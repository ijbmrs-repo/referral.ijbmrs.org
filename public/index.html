IJBMRS Referral System

This domain is used for managing and tracking official referral links associated with IJBMRS activities.

Each referral link redirects users to an official IJBMRS portal while recording anonymised, timestamped engagement data.

Important:
Referral tracking does not influence editorial decisions, peer review, acceptance, or publication outcomes. All submissions follow the same independent review process.
